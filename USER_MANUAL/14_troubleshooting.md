## 1. Login Problems

### Problem: "Invalid credentials" error
**Cause:** Incorrect Employee ID or password.  
**Solution:**
1. Verify your Employee ID number — it is your username, not your name.
2. Check that Caps Lock is not enabled.
3. Try typing your password in a text editor first to confirm it, then paste it.

---

### Problem: Account is locked
**Cause:** Too many failed login attempts triggered a lockout.  
**Solution:**
1. Do not attempt to log in again immediately — it will not work.
2. Contact your HR or IT administrator to unlock your account.
3. Once unlocked, log in with the correct credentials.

---

### Problem: Forced to change password on first login
**Cause:** Your account was flagged for a mandatory password reset.  
**Solution:**
1. This is expected behavior for new accounts or after an admin reset.
2. Enter a new password following the security requirements shown.
3. Save and you will be redirected to the dashboard.

---

### Problem: Forgot password
**Solution:**
1. Contact HR or the system administrator.
2. The admin can reset your password from the admin panel.
3. You will receive a temporary password or be prompted to set a new one.

## 2. Page Not Loading or Blank Screen

### Problem: Dashboard or module shows a blank/white page
**Cause:** Possible browser cache issue, JavaScript error, or session expiration.  
**Solution:**
1. **Hard refresh** the page: Press `Ctrl + Shift + R` (Windows) or `Cmd + Shift + R` (Mac).
2. **Clear browser cache:**
   - Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Clear Browsing Data
   - Edge: Settings > Privacy, Search and Services > Clear Browsing Data
3. Log out and log back in.
4. Try a different browser (Chrome, Edge, Firefox).

---

### Problem: Page loads but shows "Error" or "Something went wrong"
**Solution:**
1. Refresh the page (F5 or `Ctrl + R`).
2. Log out and log back in.
3. If the problem persists, note the error message and report it via an MIS ticket.

---

### Problem: Page is very slow or timing out
**Solution:**
1. Check your internet connection.
2. Close unused browser tabs and applications.
3. Try again after a few minutes.
4. If the problem persists on multiple devices, contact the MIS/IT team — it may be a server issue.

## 3. Leave Module Issues

### Problem: Cannot see the "File Leave" button
**Cause:** Your leave balance may be zero, or leave filing for your position may not be configured yet.  
**Solution:**
1. Check your leave balances — if all are zero, you may not be entitled to file.
2. Contact HR to verify your leave entitlements are correctly set up.

---

### Problem: My leave request is stuck on "Pending" for a long time
**Cause:** The assigned approver has not yet reviewed it.  
**Solution:**
1. Check which approval step is pending (visible in the request detail).
2. Politely follow up with the approver at that step.
3. If the approver is unavailable, contact HR to reassign or expedite.

---

### Problem: I cannot cancel my leave request
**Cause:** Either the 3-day cancellation window has passed, or the request is not yet fully approved.  
**Solution:**
1. If the request is still in approval steps (not yet at HR), it can only be cancelled if the current step allows it.
2. If HR has already approved it and 3 days have passed, contact HR directly to process the cancellation manually.

---

### Problem: Wrong number of leave days calculated
**Cause:** Date range may span weekends or holidays that affect the calculation.  
**Solution:**
1. Review the date range — check if Sundays should be excluded (this depends on your leave type configuration).
2. If the count is still incorrect, contact HR to verify the leave type settings.

---

## 4. Finance / Payslip Issues

### Problem: My payslip is not visible
**Cause:** Payslips are manually uploaded by accounting/admin.  
**Solution:**
1. Confirm the payroll period has been processed.
2. Contact your HR or Accounting team to check if the payslip has been uploaded.

---

### Problem: Cannot open the payslip PDF
**Cause:** Your browser may be blocking PDF opening, or the file may be corrupted.  
**Solution:**
1. Try a different browser.
2. Check if your browser has a PDF viewer enabled.
3. Download the file and open it with Adobe Acrobat or another PDF reader.
4. If the file does not open at all, report the issue to HR/accounting to re-upload.

---

### Problem: Loan balance appears incorrect
**Solution:**
1. Review your deduction history for all deductions applied.
2. If you believe there is a discrepancy, contact HR or Accounting with the specific details (dates, amounts).

---

## 5. Training & Evaluation Issues

### Problem: I cannot find my assigned training
**Cause:** You may not have been added as a participant, or the training has not been activated yet.  
**Solution:**
1. Check if the training is in **Active** status.
2. Contact your HR or supervisor to confirm you are listed as a participant.

---

### Problem: My training evaluation form is not submitting
**Cause:** Some required questions may be unanswered.  
**Solution:**
1. Scroll through the entire form and look for unanswered required questions (usually highlighted or marked with an asterisk *).
2. Complete all required fields, then retry submitting.

---

### Problem: My evaluation has been in "Supervisor Review" for a long time
**Solution:**
1. Follow up with your direct supervisor.
2. If your supervisor is unavailable, contact HR to reassign the evaluation.

---

### Problem: I accidentally submitted the wrong answers
**Solution:**
Once submitted, responses cannot be changed by the employee. Contact your supervisor or HR to explain the situation — they may be able to return the submission for re-evaluation.

---

## 6. PRF Issues

### Problem: My PRF was disapproved but I don't know why
**Solution:**
1. Open the PRF in **PR Form > My Requests**.
2. Check the **Admin Remarks** field — the administrator should have provided a reason.
3. If no remarks are present, contact HR directly for clarification.

---

### Problem: Cannot cancel a PRF
**Cause:** The PRF has already been processed (Approved or Disapproved).  
**Solution:**
1. Cancellation is only available while the PRF is **Pending**.
2. For already-approved PRFs, contact HR or accounting directly.

---

## 7. MIS Ticket Issues

### Problem: My ticket has been "Open" for a long time
**Solution:**
1. Navigate to your ticket and verify it was submitted correctly with all required information.
2. If the ticket details are incomplete, the IT team may be waiting for more information — check if there are any comments on the ticket.
3. If no response, raise the priority or contact the MIS/IT team directly.

---

### Problem: AI Chat is not responding
**Solution:**
1. Refresh the page and try again.
2. Check your internet connection.
3. If the issue persists, file a regular MIS ticket instead.

---

## 8. Profile & Photo Issues

### Problem: Cannot upload profile photo
**Cause:** File size may be too large, or the format is not supported.  
**Solution:**
1. Use JPG or PNG format.
2. Reduce the image size — try a file under 2MB.
3. If still failing, try a different browser.

---

### Problem: My profile information is incorrect (e.g., wrong department)
**Cause:** Work information is managed by HR/admin, not by the employee.  
**Solution:**
1. Contact HR or your administrator to correct your work information.
2. You can update your personal information (address, contact number, etc.) yourself via Profile Settings.

---

## 9. Notification Issues

### Problem: I am not receiving notifications
**Solution:**
1. Check if your browser has notifications blocked for the site.
2. Check the **Notification Bell** icon in the top navigation for in-app notifications — these are always available regardless of browser settings.
3. Ensure you are logged in with the correct account.

---

### Problem: I received a notification but can't find the related record
**Solution:**
1. Click the notification directly to navigate to the related module and record.
2. If it has been deleted or resolved, the notification may no longer have a target — this is normal.

## 10. General Browser Issues

### Recommended Browsers
For the best experience, use:
- **Google Chrome** (latest version)
- **Microsoft Edge** (latest version)
- **Mozilla Firefox** (latest version)

> Internet Explorer is not supported.

---

### How to Clear Browser Cache (Chrome)
1. Press `Ctrl + Shift + Delete`.
2. Set Time Range to "Last 24 hours" or "All time".
3. Check "Cached images and files" and "Cookies and other site data".
4. Click **Clear Data**.
5. Reload the RepConnect application.

---

### How to Clear Browser Cache (Edge)
1. Press `Ctrl + Shift + Delete`.
2. Set Time Range to "All time".
3. Check "Cached images and files".
4. Click **Clear now**.

---

### Session Expired — Automatically Logged Out
If you are inactive for an extended period, the system will automatically log you out for security.  
**Solution:** Simply log in again.

## Escalation Path

If the above troubleshooting steps do not resolve your issue:

```
Step 1: Try the solutions in this guide
        |
        v
Step 2: Submit an MIS Ticket (for technical/IT issues)
        |
        v
Step 3: Contact HR directly (for HR-related issues)
        |
        v
Step 4: Contact the System Administrator (for account and access issues)
```

When reporting an issue, please provide:
- A clear description of the problem
- Steps you took before the problem occurred
- Any error messages you saw (take a screenshot if possible)
- The browser and device you are using
