# 11. Certifications

## Overview

The Certifications module stores and displays official certificates issued to employees. Certificates are uploaded by administrators and organized by category. Employees receive a notification when a new certificate is added for them.

---

## How to View Your Certificates

1. Navigate to **Certifications** in the sidebar.
2. A list of your certificates appears organized by category.
3. Certificates you have **not yet opened** are marked with a **"New"** badge.
4. Click on a certificate to open and view the PDF.

> Once you open a certificate, the **"New"** badge is removed. The system records that you have viewed it.

---

## Certificate Categories

Certificates are organized into categories (e.g., Training Completion, Achievement Awards, Safety Certifications). Categories are defined by administrators.

---

## How to Upload a Certificate (Admin)

### Step 1 — Open Certification Management
Navigate to **Admin > Certifications**.

### Step 2 — Click "Upload Certificate"
Click the **Upload** or **Add Certificate** button.

### Step 3 — Fill the Form

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Employee** | Select the target employee |
| **Category** | Choose the certificate category |
| **Certificate File** | Upload the PDF certificate |
| **Certificate Name** | Provide a name/title for the certificate |

### Step 4 — Save
Click **Save**. The employee will receive a notification that a new certificate is available.

---

## Admin View

Administrators can see:
- All certificates across all employees
- Who uploaded each certificate and when
- Whether the employee has viewed the certificate

---

## Certificate View History

The system records the first time each employee opens a certificate. This allows HR to confirm that employees have received and reviewed important documents.
