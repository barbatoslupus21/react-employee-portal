# 12. Employee Profile Management

## Overview

The Employee Profile stores each employee's complete personal and professional information. Employees can update their own personal details, while HR and administrators manage work-related information.

---

## Profile Sections

```
EMPLOYEE PROFILE
├── Work Information
│   ├── Department
│   ├── Line / Section
│   ├── Office
│   ├── Shift
│   ├── Position
│   └── Employment Type (Regular, Probationary, OJT)
│
├── Personal Information
│   ├── Full Name (Last, First, Middle, Suffix)
│   ├── Nickname
│   ├── Gender
│   ├── Birthdate
│   └── Contact Number
│
├── Address
│   ├── Present Address (Street, Barangay, City, Province, Zip)
│   └── Provincial Address (or mirror from present)
│
├── Emergency Contact
│   ├── Name
│   ├── Relationship
│   └── Contact Number
│
├── Family Background
│   ├── Father's Name
│   ├── Mother's Name
│   ├── Spouse Name (if applicable)
│   └── Children (names and birthdates)
│
├── Education
│   └── (multiple) School Name, Degree, Level, Years Attended
│
└── Skills
    └── (tags/list of professional skills)
```

---

## How to Update Your Personal Information

### Step 1 — Open Profile Settings
Navigate to **Profile Settings** in the sidebar or click your name/avatar in the top-right corner.

### Step 2 — Navigate to the Section
Click the section you want to update:
- **Personal Information** — Name details, gender, birthdate, contact
- **Address** — Present and provincial address
- **Emergency Contact** — Who to call in case of emergency
- **Family Background** — Parent, spouse, and children information
- **Education** — Educational history
- **Skills** — Professional skills and competencies

### Step 3 — Edit and Save
1. Click **Edit** on the section.
2. Update the fields.
3. Click **Save**.

---

## Uploading a Profile Photo

1. Go to **Profile Settings**.
2. Click on your profile photo (or the placeholder avatar).
3. Select an image file from your device.
4. Confirm the upload.

> Supported formats: JPG, PNG. Keep the image under the maximum file size shown.

---

## Address — Mirror Provincial Address

If your present address and provincial address are the same:

1. In the **Address** section, enable the **"Same as Present Address"** toggle.
2. The provincial address fields will be automatically filled with your present address.

---

## How to Change Your Password

1. Navigate to **Profile Settings > Change Password**.
2. Enter your **current password**.
3. Enter your **new password**.
4. Confirm the new password.
5. Click **Save**.

> Passwords must meet the system's security requirements.

---

## Employee Directory (HR/Admin)

Authorized users can view and search all employees:

1. Navigate to **Employees** in the sidebar.
2. Search by name, department, position, or employment type.
3. Click on an employee to view their full profile.

HR and admins can edit work information for any employee.

---

## Employee Snapshot

The system takes daily snapshots of employee counts for historical reporting. These snapshots capture:
- Total number of employees
- Regular employees
- Probationary employees
- OJT trainees
- Gender breakdown

This information is used in HR dashboards and reports.
