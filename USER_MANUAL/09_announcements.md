# 9. Announcements

## Overview

The Announcements module is the company's internal news feed. HR, admins, and authorized news publishers can post company-wide announcements with text, images, and videos. Employees can react with emojis and leave comments.

---

## How to View Announcements

1. Navigate to **Announcements** in the sidebar.
2. Announcements are displayed as a feed, newest first.
3. Each announcement shows:
   - Title
   - Caption/body text
   - Attached images or videos
   - Emoji reactions
   - Comment count

---

## How to React to an Announcement

1. Click the emoji/reaction icon below an announcement.
2. Choose your reaction from the emoji picker.
3. Click an existing reaction to add your count or remove your reaction.

---

## How to Comment on an Announcement

1. Click the **Comment** icon or the comment section below an announcement.
2. Type your comment in the text box.
3. Press **Enter** or click **Post**.
4. To reply to a comment, click **Reply** under that comment and type your response.

---

## How to Create an Announcement (Admin/HR/News Role)

### Step 1 — Open Announcements
Navigate to **Announcements** and click **Create Announcement** or the **+** button.

### Step 2 — Fill the Form

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Title** | Announcement headline |
| **Caption** | Main body text (up to 2,000 characters) |
| **Media** | Upload images or videos (multiple allowed, drag to reorder) |
| **Status** | Draft (save for later) or Publish (live immediately) |

### Step 3 — Save or Publish
- **Save as Draft** — Visible only to admins; not shown to employees yet.
- **Publish** — Immediately visible to all employees.

---

## Managing Announcements (Admin)

- **Edit** — Click the edit icon on an announcement to update its content.
- **Unpublish** — Revert a published announcement back to draft.
- **Delete** — Permanently removes the announcement.

---

## Media Guidelines

| Type | Guidance |
|------|---------|
| Images | Common formats: JPG, PNG, GIF |
| Videos | Keep videos reasonably sized for fast loading |
| Order | Drag and drop media items to set the display order |
